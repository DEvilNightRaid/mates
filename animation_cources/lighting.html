Lighting and Rendering

Lighting and Rendering is a core skill of Computer Graphics, and one of the most important parts of any computer graphics pipeline. It encompasses all aspects of light, physics and rendering, from design to production. An understanding of lighting allows artists to craft their virtual worlds in ways that are physically plausible, photorealistic and aesthetically pleasing. From real world materials (leaves and trees) to soft shadows and skyboxes, learning how to use lighting effectively can help make your scenes look lifelike while simultaneously saving time
