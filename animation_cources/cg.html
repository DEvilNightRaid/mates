Introduction to CGI

If you're working in visual effects and animation, CGI is the process of making computers create images of things. CGI has become a big part of the industry, with 3D computer graphics software used to enhance live-action films and television shows. While CGI has some obvious benefits over traditional techniques like paint and ink, you should know that it also comes with its fair share of problems.
