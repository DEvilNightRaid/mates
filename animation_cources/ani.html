Animation

Animation is a powerful tool for telling stories, defining characters, and creating emotion. It’s also a difficult artform that requires us to rethink how we think about time. In this course, you will explore the history of animation—from its roots in early cave paintings to the innovations that helped catapult it into an artform capable of rivaling live action filmmaking. You’ll work through exercises that develop your ability to craft motion graphics as well as learn how to apply animation theory such as scale, timing and sound in programming scripts and designing storytelling animations. By the end of this course, you will understand many fundamental elements of story boarding and design creative animatics (quick sketches)
